Radioactive Waste Training

An indie dungeon crawler video game developed with a team of four at UT Austin. Play as a creature born out of radioactive waste and attempt to escape from an abandoned laboratory crawling with radioactive enemies! Features a procedurally generated map so that no two runs are exactly the same.

My role as visual director:

  • Developed original game concept and art style
  • Created all visual assets
  • Designed and implemented graphical user interface
  • Collaborated with team on gameplay design and programming

Created using the Godot 4 engine during the Fall 2023 semester for the class CS 329E: Elements of Game Development.
