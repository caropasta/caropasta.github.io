Radioactive Waste Training

An indie dungeon crawler video game developed with a team of four at UT Austin. Play as a creature born out of radioactive waste and attempt to escape from an abandoned laboratory crawling with radioactive enemies! Features a procedurally generated map so that no two runs are exactly the same. Created using the Godot 4 engine.
