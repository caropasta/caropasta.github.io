I am a current first year MS student in the Information Studies program at the University of Texas, with a focus on user experience and human-computer interaction. After earning my BS in Neuroscience from UT in May of 2024, I decided to embark on a new journey in UX research and design, inspired by my love for research, technology, and people.

I am always thinking about ways to create better interactive experiences for people on the web. Some of the UX design principles that I value the most are strong, cohesive design vision, human-centeredness, and the dual considerations of utility and joyfulness. I am also always working to improve my skills in user research, visual design, and front-end web development.
