I'm Caroline, a versatile UX designer and researcher with a background in the natural sciences.

I am a second year MS student in the Information Studies program at the University of Texas, specializing in user experience and human-computer interaction. After earning my BS in Neuroscience from UT in May of 2024, I decided to embark on a new journey in UX research and design, inspired by my love for research, technology, and people.

I am always thinking about ways to create better interactive experiences for people on the web. Some of the UX design principles that I value the most are strong, cohesive design vision, human-centeredness, and the dual considerations of utility and brand identity. Aside from UX, I also enjoy a bit of graphic design, illustration, and front-end web development.
